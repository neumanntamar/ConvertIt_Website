TEMPERATURE

Celcius to Fahrenheit

  • °F = °C ∗ 9⁄5 + 32

Kelvin to Fahrenheit

  • °F = (°K - 273.15) ∗ 9⁄5 + 32

Fahrenheit to Celcius

  • °C = (°F - 32) ∗ 5⁄9

Kelvin to Celcius

  • °C = °K - 273.15

Fahrenheit to Kelvin

  • °K = (°F - 32) ∗ 5⁄9 + 273.15

Celcius to Kelvin

  • °K = °C + 273.15
